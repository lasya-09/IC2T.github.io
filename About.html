IC2T

The International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Communication Technologies (ICICCT) serves as a premier forum for researchers, academics, industry professionals, and enthusiasts to explore the dynamic intersection of intelligent computing and communication technologies. This conference provides a platform to disseminate cutting-edge research, exchange innovative ideas, and foster collaborations that drive advancements in these rapidly evolving fields.

IC2T delves into the core aspects of intelligent systems, encompassing artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, data analytics, and their integration with modern communication networks. The conference aims to address the challenges and opportunities presented by the increasing convergence of these domains. It explores how intelligent algorithms and computational techniques can be leveraged to enhance communication networks, enabling smarter, more efficient, and robust systems. Conversely, it examines how advanced communication technologies can support and empower the development of sophisticated intelligent applications.

Key Areas of Focus

    Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
    Communication Networks
    Internet of Things (IoT)
    Cloud and Edge Computing
    Cybersecurity and Privacy
    Big Data Analytics
